Business Requirements Document (BRD)
Product Name (CAIW aka contructing AI worker)

AI Design Studio

1. Executive Summary

AI Design Studio is a desktop application that uses artificial intelligence to help users create architectural concepts, interior layouts, furniture concepts, and renovation ideas.

The platform allows users to describe a project using natural language and receive generated architectural designs, including:

Apartment layouts
Room organization
Furniture placement
Interior styles
Material suggestions
Budget constraints
Design alternatives

The system targets both non-professional users and design professionals by combining conversational AI with visual planning tools.

2. Business Problem

Architectural and interior design currently requires:

Specialized software skills
Professional knowledge
Expensive consulting
Long iteration cycles

Many users have difficulty translating ideas like:

"I want a modern 80m² apartment with a warm minimalist style"

into:

floor plans
room distribution
furniture choices
coherent designs

AI Design Studio reduces the barrier by allowing users to communicate naturally.

3. Product Vision

Create an AI design partner capable of transforming human ideas into structured architectural concepts.

Long term:

A user should be able to say:

"Design my dream house for a family of four, 180m², Mediterranean style, under $300k"

and receive:

floor plans
furniture concepts
materials
cost estimation
construction documentation

5. MVP Scope
MVP Goal

Generate a complete apartment design from a text prompt.

Example:

Input:

"Create a 90m² apartment for a couple with one child. Modern Japanese style. Large kitchen. Two bedrooms."

Output:

2D floor plan
Room allocation
Furniture placement
Style description
Material recommendations
